The Birth of a Sunday Tradition

Welcome to Namma Krishnagiri Chicken Biryani - where every grain of rice tells a story of tradition, passion, and authentic flavors.

Born in the heart of Krishnagiri, our biryani is not just a dish - it's a celebration of our rich culinary heritage. We bring you the authentic taste that has been perfected over generations.

What started as a family recipe shared during Sunday gatherings has now become a beloved tradition for biryani lovers across the region.

Why Only Sundays? ü§î

Sunday is special. It's the day when families come together, when time slows down, and when food tastes better because it's shared with loved ones.

We believe in quality over quantity. By preparing limited batches exclusively on Sundays, we ensure that every single serving gets the attention, care, and time it deserves.

Our chefs start preparing early Sunday morning, hand-picking fresh ingredients and slow-cooking each batch to perfection using the traditional dum method.
